About Brave

Brave is a non-profit organization that encourages girls STEM, specificly technology. In Brave Camp we learn how to be hungry, humble, and smart. We are taught how to use our resources to make this world a better place. We have made this app to better our understanding of this world's problems. Our team believes in being part our the solution.

In this girls only camp we are encouraged to make the best version of our selves every day. We are taught not only be in a community but how to engage and invest our selves into into the community that we have chosen to be in. The councilors have made this an incredibly fun experiance. I am very lucky to have been given this opportunity to make an app that has the potential to help people. Thank you Brave Camp. Thank you for using Santa Fe Brave.

Choose the right health insurance

This app is supposed to help you find different health insurances of New Mexico. But this app could also help you decided what health insurance you want to chose. The game below will ask you a series of questions that will find the perfect healthance plan. First just look at all the insurances that cover the doctors you want to go to. Then just look at the options that you can afford, and then see what type of health plan you would like.

Mission Statement

Santa Fe Brave is an app that brings awareness to what we belive is a huge problem with New Mexico Health insurance. The reason most people that don't have health insurance is due to the price being expremely expencive. Even with benifits health care is still very pricey. We would like you to know that you can have the safety of health insurance without the stess of overly high prices fo not full coverage.

In our interactive map you can click the city you live in, or the closest major city. This will give you a list of hospitals and the insurances nearest to you. This will give you a link to the insurance company with multiple plan options. If you have any problems feel free to contact us. Thank you for using Santa Fe Brave.
